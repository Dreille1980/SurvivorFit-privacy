Privacy Policy

SurvivorFit

Last Updated: January 20, 2025

Summary: SurvivorFit reads your Apple Health data (steps, exercise, calories) to gamify your daily activity. All data stays on your device. No information is transmitted externally. You can delete all your data at any time.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SurvivorFit. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Publisher: SurvivorFit
Contact: support@survivorfit.app

2. Data Collected

2.1 Apple Health Data (HealthKit)

SurvivorFit requests permission to read only the following data from Apple Health:

  • Step Count: to calculate your daily progress
  • Apple Exercise Time: to assess your physical activity
  • Active Energy Burned: to refine energy calculations
  • Workouts: for bonuses and badges

2.2 Game Data

The application generates and stores locally:

  • Player state (energy level, XP, streak)
  • Daily activity history (snapshots)
  • Unlocked badges
  • Preferences (custom goals, selected game mode)
  • Avatar customization

2.3 Data Not Collected

SurvivorFit DOES NOT collect:

  • Your identity (name, email, except with explicit authorization)
  • Your GPS location
  • Your contacts
  • Your photos
  • Your payment information

3. Data Usage

3.1 Purposes

Apple Health data read by SurvivorFit is used exclusively for:

  1. Gamification: Calculate your energy level, XP, and progress in game modes
  2. Motivation: Generate statistics, streaks, and badges to encourage you
  3. Visualization: Display your activity as charts and summaries

3.2 No Commercial Use

Your health data is never:

  • Sold to third parties
  • Shared with advertisers
  • Used for ad targeting
  • Transmitted to insurance companies

4. Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Important: All your data is stored locally on your iPhone/iPad via Core Data. It never leaves your device.

4.2 No External Servers

SurvivorFit operates in 100% offline mode. There is no backend server collecting your information. The app doesn't require internet connection to function (except for initial download).

4.3 Encryption

Locally stored data benefits from iOS native encryption. If your device is protected by a passcode or Face ID/Touch ID, your data is encrypted.

5. Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties. SurvivorFit contains:

  • No analytics trackers (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No social media SDKs
  • No advertising services
  • No cloud service connections

6. Your Rights

6.1 Right of Access

You can view all your data directly in the app via the History and Settings tabs.

6.2 Right to Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time:

  1. Open the SurvivorFit app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to "Data & Privacy"
  4. Tap "Reset All Data"
  5. Confirm the action

6.3 Right to Revoke HealthKit Access

To revoke SurvivorFit's access to Apple Health:

  1. Open Apple's Health app
  2. Tap your profile picture in the top right
  3. Select "Privacy"
  4. Tap "SurvivorFit"
